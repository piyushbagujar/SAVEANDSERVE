About Us

At SAVE AND SERVE, our mission is to rescue surplus food and deliver it to those who need it the most. Every day, perfectly good food goes to waste while millions struggle with hunger. We are here to change that narrative.

Our platform connects food donors—including restaurants, households, and businesses—with receivers such as NGOs, shelters, and underprivileged communities. We strive to make the donation process simple, safe, and efficient.
